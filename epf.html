What is EPF Withdrawal?

EPF allows employees to save for retirement. If needed, employees can withdraw their funds under certain conditions.

Who Can Withdraw?

  • Employees who have retired
  • Individuals who have been unemployed for more than 2 months
  • Those withdrawing funds for medical or educational purposes
