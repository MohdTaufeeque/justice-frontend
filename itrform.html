What is the ITR Form?

The Income Tax Return (ITR) form is a document used by taxpayers to declare their earnings and pay taxes accordingly. It helps individuals and businesses remain compliant with tax regulations.

Who Needs to File?

  • Individuals with an annual income above the tax-free limit
  • Businesses and firms
  • Freelancers and self-employed professionals
