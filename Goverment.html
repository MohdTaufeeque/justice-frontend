What is NSP?

The National Scholarship Portal (NSP) is a platform for students to apply for various government scholarships and financial aid.

Who Can Apply?

  • Students from economically weaker sections
  • Meritorious students seeking financial aid
  • Minority, SC/ST, and OBC students eligible for government scholarships
