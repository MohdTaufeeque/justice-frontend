What are State Legal Services Authorities?

State Legal Services Authorities (SLSA) work under the National Legal Services Authority (NALSA) to provide free legal aid, organize Lok Adalats, and promote legal awareness among citizens.

Key Services

  • Free legal aid for economically weaker sections
  • Lok Adalats for out-of-court settlements
  • Legal awareness and assistance programs
