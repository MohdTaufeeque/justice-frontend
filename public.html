Who is a Public Defender?

Public defenders are court-appointed attorneys who provide free legal representation to individuals who cannot afford a private lawyer.

How to Apply?

  • Check eligibility for free legal services.
  • Find a public defender in your state.
  • Submit an application through the court system.
